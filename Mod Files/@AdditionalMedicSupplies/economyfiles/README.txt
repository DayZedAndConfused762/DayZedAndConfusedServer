How to install the economy files:

1. Copy the <type> entries from db/types_AMS.xml to your db/types.xml
2. Copy the <type> entries from cfgspawnabletypes_AMS.xml to your cfgspawnabletypes.xml
3. Copy the category amsmedchest from cfglimitsdefintion_AMS.xml to your cfglimitsdefintion.xml
4. Copy the <dispatch> entries from the buildings in mapgroupproto_AMS.xml to same buildings in your mapgroupproto.xml
5. Make adjustments to the entries as needed (types.xml and cfgspawnabletypes.xml)
